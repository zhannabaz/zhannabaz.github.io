About me

I'm a Registered Psychotherapist (Qualifying) offering online therapy in Canada.

My approach is warm, collaborative, and grounded in helping you better understand yourself - not fix you.

I aim to create a space where you can slow down, reflect, and reconnect with what matters to you.

I bring an understanding of experiences such as anxiety, overwhelm, life transitions, and adapting to change.

How I can help

I work with individuals and couples experiencing:

  • Anxiety and overthinking Support for spiraling thoughts, tension, and emotional overload.
  • Relationship challenges Space to understand patterns, communication, and disconnection.
  • Life transitions Support through change, uncertainty, and shifting identity.
  • Feeling overwhelmed or stuck A calmer place to slow down and find your next step.

Professional information

Registered Psychotherapist (Qualifying)

I work under the supervision of an experienced licensed therapist. Supervision is an important part of ethical practice and helps ensure thoughtful and responsible care.

I am currently in the process of completing the requirements for full licensure.
